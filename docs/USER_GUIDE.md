## What is GRAIVE?

GRAIVE is an online course platform that teaches kids aged 10–18 how to think with AI — not just use it.

Each course level is built around a core question:

| Level | Ages | Core Skill |
|-------|------|-----------|
| Explorer | 10–11 | What do I want from AI? |
| Builder | 12–13 | How do I explain my idea clearly? |
| Thinker | 14–15 | How do I guide AI step by step? |
| Innovator | 16–18 | How do I build systems with AI? |

Every module includes:
- A lesson to read at your own pace
- A built-in AI tutor you can chat with
- Hands-on activities to try
- Reflection questions

**Explorer Level is completely free.** Builder, Thinker, and Innovator require a Pro subscription.

## Getting Started

### 1. Create an account

Go to the homepage and click **Start Free**.

You'll be asked to:
- Enter your email and create a password (minimum 8 characters)
- Choose your age group — this sets your starting level
- Enter a parent or guardian email if you're aged 10–13 (required)

> **Note:** If you're a parent signing up your child, use the child's age group when selecting a tier.

### 2. Confirm your email

Check your inbox for a confirmation email and click the link. You won't be able to log in until you've confirmed.

### 3. Go to your Dashboard

Once logged in, you'll land on your **Dashboard**. This shows:
- Your current level and progress
- All your modules with completion status
- Quick links to continue where you left off

## Choosing Your Level

When you sign up, you pick your age group. This determines your starting level:

- **Ages 10–11 → Explorer** (free)
- **Ages 12–13 → Builder** (Pro)
- **Ages 14–15 → Thinker** (Pro)
- **Ages 16–18 → Innovator** (Pro)

You can see all four levels from your Dashboard — locked levels show a padlock and a link to upgrade.

> **Can I change my level?**
> Contact support. Level changes are possible but require admin approval to keep the learning progression meaningful.

## Working Through a Module

### Opening a module

From your Dashboard or the level overview page, click any module card to open it.

### The module page layout

On a **desktop or tablet**, you'll see a split view:

```
┌────────────────────────────────┬──────────────────┐
│  LESSON CONTENT                │  AI TUTOR CHAT   │
│                                │                  │
│  • Lesson text                 │  [Chat history]  │
│  • Activities to try           │                  │
│  • Reflection questions        │  [Type here...]  │
└────────────────────────────────┴──────────────────┘
```

On a **phone**, the lesson and chat are in separate tabs — tap **Lesson** or **AI Chat** at the top to switch.

### Reading the lesson

Work through the lesson from top to bottom. Each lesson is structured as:

1. **Objective** — what you'll be able to do after this
2. **What You'll Need** — tools and time required
3. **Activities** — things to try (usually 2–3 per module)
4. **Reflect** — questions to think about or write down
5. **Coming Up Next** — a teaser for the next module

### Marking complete

When you've finished a module, click the **Mark Complete** button at the top of the page. This saves your progress and unlocks the next module.

You'll automatically be taken to the next module after a moment.

> **Tip:** You can mark a module complete even if you haven't done every activity. Come back to activities any time — your progress is saved.

## Using the AI Tutor

Every module has a built-in AI tutor — **Spark** for Explorer level, **Sage** for all other levels.

The tutor knows which module you're on and will help you with the lesson content.

### How to chat

1. Type your question or message in the box at the bottom of the chat panel
2. Press **Enter** to send (or click the send button)
3. Press **Shift + Enter** to add a new line without sending

### What the AI tutor can help with

- Explaining concepts from the lesson in a different way
- Answering "what if" questions about the topic
- Helping you with activities if you're stuck
- Giving feedback on your prompt attempts
- Going deeper on something that interests you

### What the AI tutor won't do

The tutor is scoped to the lesson topic. It won't:
- Help with unrelated homework or tasks
- Discuss topics outside the module subject (especially on Explorer level)
- Provide personal advice or act as a friend/companion

> **Explorer level:** The tutor has stricter limits and keeps responses short and simple — this is intentional for the 10–11 age group.

### Free vs Pro message limits

- **Free (Explorer):** 10 AI messages per day. The counter resets at midnight.
- **Pro (all levels):** Unlimited messages.

If you hit your daily limit, you'll see a message letting you know. It resets the next day, or you can upgrade to Pro for unlimited access.

### Conversation history

If history is enabled (it is by default), your previous chats in each module are saved. When you return to a module, you'll see your earlier conversation.

## Tracking Your Progress

### Dashboard overview

Your Dashboard shows:
- A progress bar for your current level
- How many modules you've completed out of the total
- Individual module cards marked with a green tick when complete

### Level overview page

Click **Continue Learning** from the Dashboard, or navigate to your level from the top menu.

The level overview shows all modules in order with:
- Module number and title
- Estimated time
- Completion status
- An **Up Next** badge on the module you should do next

### Can I skip ahead?

Yes — modules don't lock each other out. You can open any module in your level at any time. The suggested order is there to help, not restrict.

## Upgrading to Pro

Explorer Level is free forever. To access Builder, Thinker, or Innovator:

1. Go to **Account → Billing** (click your name in the top right)
2. Choose **Monthly ($9.99/month)** or **Annual ($79.99/year — 2 months free)**
3. You'll be taken to a secure Stripe checkout page
4. After payment, Pro access is activated immediately

### Managing your subscription

- Go to **Account → Billing** and click **Manage Subscription**
- This opens Stripe's Customer Portal where you can change plan, update payment details, or cancel
- Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period

### What if I'm locked out of a paid tier?

If your subscription lapses, you'll be redirected to the billing page. Your progress is saved — it'll all be there when you resubscribe.

## For Parents

### Who can see my child's data?

- Only your child can see their conversations and progress (protected by Row Level Security)
- We do not share or sell data with third parties
- Children aged 10–13 require a parent email at signup — this is used for account-related communications only, not marketing

### AI safety for younger learners

The **Explorer level** (ages 10–11) has strict AI guardrails:
- The tutor only discusses the current lesson topic
- Responses are kept short and age-appropriate (max ~150 words)
- Off-topic questions are redirected back to the lesson
- No adult content is possible within this tier

Older tiers gradually open up as learners advance — Builder allows broader digital literacy topics, Thinker opens ethics and society discussions, Innovator is nearly fully open with standard AI safety only.

### Subscription management

Parents can manage billing at **Account → Billing**. The Stripe Customer Portal gives full control over payment methods and cancellation.

### Contact

For account issues, questions about a child's experience, or data requests, contact us through the platform's support page.

## Troubleshooting

### I can't log in

- Check you confirmed your email after signing up (look for the confirmation email)
- Try the **Forgot Password** link on the sign-in page
- Make sure you're using the email address you signed up with

### The AI chat isn't responding

- Check your internet connection
- If you're on the free tier, check whether you've hit your daily 10-message limit
- Try refreshing the page — if the issue persists, try a different browser

### My progress didn't save

- Make sure you clicked **Mark Complete** — just reading the module doesn't auto-save
- If the button gave an error, refresh and try again
- Check your internet connection

### The lesson content isn't loading

- Try a hard refresh (Ctrl + F5 on Windows, Cmd + Shift + R on Mac)
- If a module shows "Content coming soon", it's been scheduled but not yet published — check back soon

### I can't access a paid tier despite having a subscription

- Your subscription may have a payment issue — go to Account → Billing to check status
- If billing shows active but access is still blocked, try signing out and back in
- Contact support with your account email if the issue persists
