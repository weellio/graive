# Builder Level — Ages 12–13
## Theme: Understanding the Machine

---

### Who This Is For
Kids aged 12–13 who are curious about how AI actually works — not just "it's magic" but the real mechanics behind it. They use TikTok, YouTube, and maybe ChatGPT already. This tier meets them where they are and takes them deeper.

### The Big Idea
You're already surrounded by algorithms. This tier teaches you to understand them, question them, and *use* them on purpose instead of being used by them.

### Core Skill by End of Tier
Write a structured prompt using the RCTFC framework (Role, Context, Task, Format, Constraints) that produces a genuinely useful AI output on the first or second attempt.

### Capstone
Build a "Study Buddy" chatbot — give it a subject, a personality, and rules for what it should and shouldn't do. Test it by actually using it to study for something real.

---

### Prompt Engineering Progression: CLARITY
> "How do I explain my idea clearly, with enough context that AI can actually help me?"

Kids will learn that AI isn't a mind reader. The quality of the output is 100% dependent on the quality of the input. By the end of this tier, they'll instinctively ask: *"Did I give it enough to work with?"*
