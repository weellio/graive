# Capstone Project: The Prompt Cooking Show
### Explorer Level (Ages 10–11) | Final Project

---

## What You're Building

A "before and after" showcase of your best prompt work — showing how a vague prompt becomes an amazing one.

This is your chance to show off everything you've learned. No new skills needed — just apply what you've got.

---

## The Prompt Cooking Show Format

You're the host. The audience is anyone who watches or reads your project.

Your show has three parts:

### Part 1: The Before (The Terrible Prompt)
Write the worst possible prompt for your topic — vague, no context, no format, no style.
Run it. Show the output. Explain what's wrong with both the prompt and the output.

### Part 2: The Cooking (Building the Recipe)
Walk through building the proper prompt, ingredient by ingredient:
- Context first: "I added context because..."
- Instruction next: "I was specific because..."
- Format last: "I chose this format because..."
- Also use the Three S's: Setting, Stakes, Style

Show how you iterated too — at least one improvement from the first draft.

### Part 3: The After (The Amazing Output)
Show your final output. Explain what's different. What made it better?

## The Cooking Show Script

Write out your show like a script. You can make it simple — just answer each part.

```
WELCOME:
"Hi, I'm [NAME] and today on the Prompt Cooking Show
I'm going to show you how to make [YOUR TOPIC] amazing with AI."

THE TERRIBLE PROMPT:
"Here's what most people would type: [TERRIBLE PROMPT]"
"And here's what AI gives them: [PASTE OUTPUT]"
"The problems with this are: [EXPLAIN]"

THE RECIPE:
"Here's how I cooked up a better version.
First, I added Context: [YOUR CONTEXT] — because [REASON]
Then, my Instruction: [YOUR INSTRUCTION] — because [REASON]
Then, the Format: [YOUR FORMAT] — because [REASON]
For the Three S's: [SETTING / STAKES / STYLE]"

THE FIRST DRAFT:
"Here's what I got first: [PASTE OUTPUT]"
"I improved it by: [YOUR IMPROVEMENT]"

THE FINAL RESULT:
"Here's my final output: [PASTE IT]"
"The biggest difference from the terrible prompt is: [EXPLAIN]"
```
