# Lesson Plan: Create with AI
### Explorer Level (Ages 10–11) | Module 06

---

## Objective
By the end of this lesson, you have created something genuinely fun and original using AI, demonstrating mastery of specific prompts, the recipe, and iteration.

---

## What You'll Need
- AI tool
- Notebook or document to save your prompts and outputs
- 45–60 minutes (this is the big creative session!)

---

## Watch First
Watch **Module 06: Create with AI** — the most fun video in the course.

## Try It — The Creative Studio

### Warm-Up: Try the Three Moves (15 mins)

Quickly try each creative move from the video:

**Move 1 — The Combination:**
Pick two unexpected things to combine. Write a short prompt (2–3 sentences) and run it.

My combination: _______ + _______

**Move 2 — The Twist:**
Pick something familiar and ask "what if?" Write and run it.

My twist: What if _______________________?

**Move 3 — The Voice Swap:**
Tell something from an unexpected point of view. Run it.

My voice swap: _______ from the perspective of _______

**Which of the three got the best result?**

---

### Main Event: Make Your Masterpiece (30–40 mins)

Now create ONE thing you're really proud of. Choose your project type:

| Option | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| 🐉 Short Story | 1 page, with a character, a problem, and an ending |
| 🎤 Song Lyrics | A verse and chorus about something you love |
| 📰 Fake News | A funny headline + first 3 paragraphs |
| 🎮 Game Concept | A game name, how you win, one special rule |
| 📢 Product Ad | Invent a silly product and write its advertisement |
| 🐦 Nature Documentary | Your pet / a family member narrated by Attenborough |

**Step 1: Write your first prompt using the Recipe**

```
CONTEXT: ___________________________________________
INSTRUCTION: _______________________________________
FORMAT: ____________________________________________
```

**Step 2: Run it. Read it. What would make it better?**

Write your improvement idea: _______________________

**Step 3: Second prompt (improve one specific thing)**

**Step 4: Run it again. What would make it even better?**

Write your improvement idea: _______________________

**Step 5: Third prompt. Final version.**

**Your final output:** (copy it in here or paste it)

---

### The Show-Off Step

Read your finished piece to someone — a family member, a friend, anyone. Watch their reaction.

Did they enjoy it? Did they laugh? Did they ask questions?

**Their reaction:** _______________________

---

## Reflect
1. How many times did you iterate before you were happy with the result?
2. What part of the creative process was YOU and what part was the AI?
3. Did the AI surprise you in a good way at any point? What happened?
