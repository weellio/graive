# Innovator Level — Ages 16–18
## Theme: Build with AI, Think Beyond AI

---

### Who This Is For
16–18 year olds who are thinking seriously about the future — university, careers, starting something. They want practical, advanced skills that will actually matter. They're ready for philosophical depth and real-world complexity. Treat them as adults.

### The Big Idea
You're entering the workforce and higher education at the exact moment AI is reshaping both. The people who thrive won't just know how to use AI — they'll know how to build with it, think critically about it, and take responsibility for it.

### Core Skill by End of Tier
Build a working multi-step AI automation or complex prompting workflow, and articulate a reasoned position on an AI governance challenge.
