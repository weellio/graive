# Capstone Project: Build Your Study Buddy
### Builder Level (Ages 12–13) | Final Project

---

## What You're Building

A custom AI-powered Study Buddy — a chatbot you've deliberately designed, tested, and refined to help you learn better.

This isn't about making a flashy product. It's about applying everything from this tier:
- Your understanding of how AI works (Module 01)
- Structured prompting with RCTFC (Module 02)
- Awareness of algorithms and misinformation (Modules 03–04)
- Persona design with a system prompt (Module 05)
- Privacy and responsible use (Module 06)

---

## The Brief

**Design a Study Buddy chatbot for your worst or most challenging subject.**

Your Study Buddy should:
- Know what subject it's for and at what level
- Quiz you rather than just give you answers
- Give hints, not solutions
- Adapt to how you're feeling (frustrated? celebrate small wins)
- Stay in character and not help you shortcut the learning

## Step 5: Reflect on the Full Process (20 mins)

Write a short summary (one paragraph each) on:

1. **What I built and how it works** — describe your Study Buddy and what makes it work.

2. **What I learned from building it** — what was harder than you expected? What clicked?

3. **How understanding AI's patterns (Module 01) helped me build this** — connect your knowledge of how AI works to the choices you made in the persona.

4. **If I were going to sell this to other students** — what would the pitch be? What's the unique value of *your* Study Buddy compared to just using ChatGPT normally?
