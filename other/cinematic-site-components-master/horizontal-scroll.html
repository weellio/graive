Why it works

Gallery without a carousel

Carousels have terrible engagement. Horizontal scroll hijack solves the same problem — showing many items in sequence — but uses the visitor's natural scroll gesture instead of tiny arrow buttons. Agencies, portfolios, and process timelines all convert better with this pattern.
