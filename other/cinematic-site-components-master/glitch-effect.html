Module 28 — Glitch Effect

RGB channel split on hover. Digital distortion.

GLITCH

Cyberpunk aesthetic

RGB channel splitting with clip-path creates a digital distortion effect. Pure CSS, no canvas needed.

Hover-triggered

The glitch fires only on hover. Constant glitching is distracting — controlled bursts create drama.

Scanline overlay

CRT-style scanlines on the cards add to the digital aesthetic. A 4px repeating gradient at low opacity.

Zero JavaScript

The entire effect is CSS keyframes with clip-path and translate. GPU-accelerated, no layout thrashing.

Why it works

Controlled chaos

The glitch effect signals edge, disruption, and technical confidence. Gaming, music, cybersecurity, and tech-forward brands use it to stand out from clean corporate sites. The key is restraint — a brief hover burst, not constant noise.
