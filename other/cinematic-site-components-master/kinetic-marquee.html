Why it works

Energy without effort

Marquees fill horizontal space that headlines can't. They create ambient motion that makes a page feel dynamic even when the visitor isn't interacting. The scroll-reactive speed adds a tactile layer — the page responds to how fast you're scrolling, which feels alive. Client logos, tech stacks, service lists, and brand statements all work here. Used heavily by agencies, portfolios, and SaaS landing pages.
