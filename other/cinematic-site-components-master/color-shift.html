Module 09 — Scroll Color Shift

The page changes colour as you scroll

Background and text transition smoothly between palettes as each section enters view. Creates distinct moods for each chapter of your story.

Warm

Chapter one

A warm amber palette signals craft, heritage, and trust. Bakeries, jewellers, and luxury brands feel at home here.

Forest

Chapter two

Deep green communicates growth, health, and sustainability. Fintech, wellness, and environmental brands gravitate here.

Night

Chapter three

Cool violet carries creativity, innovation, and premium tech. SaaS, AI products, and creative studios own this space.

Light

Chapter four

Flipping to light mode mid-page is a power move. It resets attention and signals a shift in tone — from storytelling to action.

Return

Back to dark

Returning to the original palette closes the loop. The visitor feels a complete journey, not a random collection of sections.

Why it works

Mood per chapter

Colour is the fastest way to change emotional context. Each section becomes its own world with its own feeling. Long-form brand pages, annual reports, and multi-product sites use this to tell stories with distinct chapters without breaking the single-page flow.
