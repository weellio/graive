Why it works

Proof through interaction

Before/after is the most persuasive visual format for any transformation business — renovations, detailing, design, fitness, cosmetics. The cursor-driven reveal lets the visitor control the comparison, which creates agency and trust. They're not watching your marketing, they're discovering the difference themselves. Four variants cover different use cases: wipe for direct comparison, spotlight for dramatic reveals, vertical for landscape shots, hover grid for portfolios.
